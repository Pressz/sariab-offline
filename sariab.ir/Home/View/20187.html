Visard Org

Visard is a Bangalore based non-profit, established by high school students in 2021 while local businesses continue to be impacted by the Covid 19 pandemic. With the intent to support local businesses and charities, we provide highly subsidized technology services to businesses and further donate 100%* of generated proceeds to charities.
